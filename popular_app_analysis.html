Popular App Analysis

By: Garrett von Ting | Published: 2021-11-18

I am in a club called Nittany Data Labs at Penn State University. As a member of this club we are given a premium subscription to the Dataquest platform (dataquest.io). Since I was given this subscription I wanted to take advantage of it. Dataquest has different paths to take and to start off I have chosen to take the Data Scientist in Python path. Thanks to my own personal projects I was experienced in python, but this is the first time I have touched the Jupyter notebook platform. After using Jupyter I have found that it is a good format to explain what the code is doing to the data, and why you are doing it.

This is the first project that was given to me through the Dataquest platform. I was tasked with analyzing the app data from the Apple iOS Store and the Google Play Store to find where a software development company should focus their development efforts.
